Privacy Policy for DailyLift

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to DailyLift ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application DailyLift - Quotes & Motivation (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

DailyLift is designed with your privacy in mind. The App:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, camera, or other device features
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not display advertisements

Data Storage

All app data (such as quotes) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit any data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Sharing Feature

When you use the "Share" feature, the quote text is shared using your device's native sharing functionality. We do not have access to or store any information about your sharing activities.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
